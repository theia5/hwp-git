Residential Asphalt and Concrete

Residential concrete and asphalt projects are very special to us at Hennepin West Paving & Concrete. As homeowners ourselves we know how exciting it is to get a brand new driveway, or watch that dream patio become reality. It gives us a special feeling to see the look of pride in the faces of a family as they look at their new steps or smooth sidewalk. From steps, stoops, and driveways to aprons, garage floors and patios, no concrete or asphalt project is too big or too small for Hennepin West Paving & Concrete.

Our professionals offer you their vast knowledge and experience in deciding whether your steps, driveway, sidewalk, patio, garage floor or apron can best be patched and repaired, or whether full replacement will serve your needs better. We are very conscious of the fact that you work hard for your money, so we always give you high quality craftsmanship and materials at an affordable price.

If you are considering a concrete or asphalt project at your home, please give us a call to schedule a free consultation and estimate - it would be our pleasure to work with you!
