About Hennepin West Paving & Concrete

After many years of learning the trade and perfecting his skills in the paving industry, Randy Ries set out on his own and started Hennepin West Paving & Concrete, Inc. It was then, and it still is now, a family owned business dedicated to excellence and customer service.

Much has changed since that first day in 1994! Paving equipment, materials, and techniques have improved, the customer base and number of employees has grown, other paving contractors have come and gone and Randy is that much older. But the things that have not changed, and never will, are Hennepin West Paving & Concrete's commitment to high quality workmanship, use of the finest materials available, and attentive customer service.

Whatever your paving needs might be we are equipped to handle them. From parking lots and driveways, to patios, sidewalks, and garage floors we have the skill, equipment and experience to do the job right. In fact, Hennepin West Paving & Concrete has recently received the coveted “A Plus Rating” from the Better Business Bureau.

Hennepin West Paving & Concrete offers free consultations and estimates on any project you might be planning. We will lend our professional experience in helping you determine whether a driveway or parking lot or other paving project could be patched, repaired and seal coated, or if it should be totally replaced.

Our pride in workmanship is second to none, and that sets us apart in the paving industry. At Hennepin West Paving & Concrete, the bottom line is stated in customer satisfaction and not dollars and cents. Our work always exceeds state requirements; we offer the best warranty in the business; we never exceed your budget and we finish the work on time with a minimum amount of inconvenience to you.

Please give us a call and allow us to show you what we can do!
